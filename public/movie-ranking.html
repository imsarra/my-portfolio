The Best Movies According to Sarah

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Synecdoche, New York (2008)

My all-time favorite, I really enjoy Charlie Kaufman's work and how he deals with thems of death and the passage of time.

2. In The Mood for Love (2000)

A great piece by the great Wong Kar-Wai, he manipulates colors and music to share with the viewer this concept of taboo love
We already know our two protagonasts (?) won't end up together, yet we still hold to that glimps of hope or just try to enjpy the ride while it lasts

3. I'm Thinking of Ending Things (2020)

Another beautiful film by Kaufman, it deals with similar themes to his 2008 feature but this one is a bit more abstract (I guess all his films are?) in the sense that we follow the story of a ganitor as he reflects on his "youth" through the eyes of an opportunity he never approched
This guy is so misrable that even his mind could not give him the happy imagination of people acceptance ??
